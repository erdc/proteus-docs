Detailed Description

Nonlinear advection-diffusion reaction (fully-coupled, multi-component systems)

The equations have the form

\[ m^i + \nabla \cdot \left( \mathbf{f}^i + \sum_{j=i}^{nc-1} \mathbf{a}^i \nabla u^j \right) + r^i = 0 \\ \]

where \(i,j=0,\ldots,cn-1\) and

\begin{eqnarray*} m^i &=& M^i (u^i)^p + \sum_{j \neq i} \epsilon m^j \\ f^i &=& \mathbf{b}^i (u^i)^q + \sum_{j \neq i} \epsilon f^j \\ a^{i,i} &=& A^i (u^i)^t \\ a^{i,j} &=& \epsilon \mathbf{a}^{i,i} \quad j \neq i \\ \phi^i &=& (u^i)^r \\ r^i &=& c^i (u^i)^s + \sum_{j \neq i} \epsilon r^j \end{eqnarray*} 

This class implements the simple nonlinear ADR equation with full coupling
