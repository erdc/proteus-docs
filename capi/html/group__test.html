int la_gauss_2d_p.nd = 2

Conservative linear advection of a cone in a rotating velocity field.

\begin{eqnarray*} \phi_t + \nabla \cdot (\vec u \phi) &=& 0 \\ \Omega &=& [0,1] \times [0,1] \\ u^{x} &=& 2\pi(y-1/2)\\ u^{y} &=& 2\pi(1/2-x)\\ \phi^{ex}(x,y,t) &=& \exp\left(-\frac{\|\vec x - \vec x_c\|^2}{2\sigma^2}\right) \end{eqnarray*}

where \(\bar{x} = x - x_c\), \(\bar{y} = y - y_c\), and \( x_c = \sin(2\pi t)/4 + 1/2\), \(\; y_c = \cos(2\pi t)/4 + 1/2 \)

exact solution, T=0.75
RKDG P^2 solution, Cr=0.15, L^2 error= 8.3e-4
