Detailed Description

Manages the DOF for interlaced velocity and pressure ordering.

Notes
-----
Interlaced degrees of occur when the degrees of freedom are
ordered as (p[0], u[0], v[0], p[1], u[1], ..., p[n], u[n], v[n]).
