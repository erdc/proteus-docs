Detailed Description

Incompressible Navier-Stokes equations.

The equations are formulated as

\begin{eqnarray*} \nabla \cdot \mathbf{v} &=& 0 \\ \frac{\partial \left \mathbf{v}\right }{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot \left(\mathbf{v} \otimes \mathbf{v} - \nu \Delta \mathbf{f} \right) + \frac{\nabla p}{\rho}- \mathbf{g} &=& 0 \end{eqnarray*}

where \(\mathbf{v}\) is the velocity, \(p\) is the pressure, \(\nu\) is the kinematic viscosity, \(\rho\) is the density, and \(\mathbf{g}\) is the gravitational acceleration. 

The coefficients for the incompressible Navier-Stokes equations.
